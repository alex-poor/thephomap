## Methodology

- **Data sources:** NZ HPI facility register (Jan 2026), PHO enrollment data, SA3 census demographics with NZ Deprivation Index, Healthpoint.co.nz (scraped for hours/contact details)
- **Spatial join:** Each GP practice point matched to its containing SA3 statistical area using point-in-polygon
- **After-hours distance:** Straight-line (haversine) distance to nearest community after-hours clinic (109 sites) — actual travel distances will be longer
- **ED distance:** Straight-line distance to nearest hospital Emergency Department (34 sites)
- **Deprivation:** NZ Deprivation Index 2018, averaged at SA3 level (1 = least deprived, 10 = most deprived)
- **Ethnicity %:** Māori and Pacific peoples as percentage of SA3 total population (Census 2023)
- **Enrolled:** Enrolled Service Users (ESUs) from PHO register — a weighted measure of practice workload

*Raw data available in `gp-summary.csv` for further analysis.*
